Tip 1: Use quotes to search for an exact phrase

  • This one’s a well-known, simple trick: searching a phrase in quotes will yield only pages with the same words in the same order as what’s in the quotes.
  • It’s one of the most vital search tips, especially useful if you’re trying to find results containing a specific a phrase.
